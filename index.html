"Identifying Trends and Market Opportunities in the Service Industry

This project uses the Yelp Dataset to predict trends and future business attention, and identify potentially lucrative business locations for different business categories. First, heirarchial clustering is performed to cluster geographic clusters of businesses. Then, the Random Forests regression model is used for predicting future business attention, and association rule mining is done to dentify complementary business categories. Finally, clusters with a large number of complementary businesses and low number of competing businesses are identified, which indicate potential business opportunities.

Python, PySpark, R, pandas
